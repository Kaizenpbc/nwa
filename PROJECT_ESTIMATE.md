## 5. Assumptions & Notes

1. **Rate**: All costs calculated at a blended rate of $35.00 USD/hour across all roles.
2. **Work week**: 40 hours/week during implementation; reduced hours during post-launch support.
3. **Post-launch support**: 2 months with PM at ~15 hrs/week and developer on-call at ~10 hrs/week for bug fixes, security patches, and minor enhancements per SLA terms.
4. **Scope**: Based on ~30 core pages at go-live, 21 deliverables per TOR, 65 functional specification items, and 10+ app integration modules.
5. **NWA responsibilities**: Server provisioning, content delivery (50%), branding assets, UAT team, and milestone sign-offs must be provided on schedule. Delays in NWA deliverables may impact timeline and cost.
6. **Excludes**: Financial proposal preparation costs, travel expenses, hardware/hosting (NWA-provided), third-party software licenses (none required — open-source stack), and any scope changes approved through the Change Control process.
7. **Currency**: Estimate is in USD. The RFP requires Financial Proposal submission in Jamaican Dollars (JMD). Convert at prevailing exchange rate for FIN-1/FIN-2/FIN-3 forms.
8. **Contingency**: No contingency buffer is included. Consider adding 10–15% ($12,040–$18,060) for scope uncertainties, NWA-side delays, or additional UAT cycles.
